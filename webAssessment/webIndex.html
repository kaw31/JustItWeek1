WELCOME TO FDG FORENSIC INVESTIGATORS

FDG Forensics is a renowned Partnership of forensic scientists and engineers specialising in the investigation of fires, explosions, engineering failures and other incidents. The organisation provides expert witness services to the legal, insurance and commercial sectors.
